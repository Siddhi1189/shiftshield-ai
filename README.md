# 🚀 ShiftShield AI

### AI-Powered Income Protection for Gig Workers

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

India’s gig delivery workers (Zomato, Swiggy, etc.) lose **20–30% of their income** due to external disruptions such as:

* Heavy rain
* Floods
* Pollution (AQI spikes)
* Traffic restrictions

These events are **unpredictable and uncontrollable**, and currently, there is **no real-time financial safety net**.

---

## 👤 Target Persona

**Food Delivery Rider**

* Platform: Zomato / Swiggy
* Work hours: Evening shifts (5 PM – 10 PM)
* Weekly income: ₹4000–₹5000

### 🎯 Pain Point

Loss of income due to factors beyond their control.

---

## 💡 Solution

ShiftShield AI is an **AI-powered parametric insurance platform** that acts as:

> ⚡ A real-time decision + protection system for gig workers

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

```plaintext
User Login → AI Risk Analysis → Policy Generation
→ Trigger Monitoring → Auto Claim → Instant Payout
```

1. Worker logs into platform
2. AI calculates risk score (weather + AQI)
3. Weekly insurance policy is generated
4. System monitors disruption triggers
5. Claim is automatically triggered
6. Fraud checks are applied
7. Instant payout is simulated

---

## 🧠 AI Integration

### 🔹 Risk Prediction

* Uses rainfall + AQI
* Outputs disruption probability

### 🔹 Dynamic Pricing

* Premium adjusts based on risk level
* Reflects real-time environmental conditions

### 🔹 Shift Optimization (USP 🚀)

* Suggests **stay vs relocate strategy**
* Maximizes worker earnings

### 🔹 Fraud Detection

* Movement patterns
* Activity logs
* Multi-signal validation

---

## ⭐ Key Innovation — Safe Zone Recommendation

ShiftShield doesn’t just insure — it **prevents loss**.

* Suggests safer working areas
* Helps workers optimize earnings
* Moves from reactive → proactive system

---

## 📊 Parametric Triggers

Claims are triggered automatically when:

* Rainfall > 80–100mm
* AQI > 250–300
* Flood alert detected
* Traffic disruption occurs
