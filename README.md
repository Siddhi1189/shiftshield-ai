# 🚀 ShiftShield AI  
### AI-Powered Income Protection for Gig Workers  

---

## 📌 Problem Statement  

India’s gig delivery workers (Zomato, Swiggy, etc.) lose **20–30% of their income** due to external disruptions such as:

- Heavy rain  
- Floods  
- Pollution  
- Traffic restrictions  

These events are **unpredictable and uncontrollable**, and currently, there is **no financial safety net** to protect their earnings.

---

## 👤 Target Persona  

**Primary Persona: Food Delivery Rider**

- Platform: Zomato / Swiggy  
- Work hours: Evening shifts (5 PM – 10 PM)  
- Weekly income: ₹4000–₹5000  

### Pain Point  
Loss of income due to disruptions beyond their control.

---

## 💡 Our Solution  

**ShiftShield AI** is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform that acts as an:

> **AI assistant for gig workers**

### It enables:

- Prediction of income loss risk before shifts  
- Weekly insurance coverage aligned with earning cycles  
- Automatic claim triggering based on real-world events  
- Instant compensation for lost income  

---

## ⚙️ How It Works  

1. Worker registers on the platform  
2. AI calculates a **risk score** using external data  
3. Weekly insurance policy is generated  
4. System monitors disruption signals  
5. Claim is automatically triggered  
6. Fraud checks are applied  
7. Instant payout is processed  

---

## 🧠 AI Integration  

ShiftShield AI leverages AI across multiple layers:

### 🔹 Risk Prediction  
- Uses weather, AQI, and traffic data  
- Predicts probability of disruption  

### 🔹 Dynamic Pricing  
- Adjusts weekly premium based on risk score  

### 🔹 Fraud Detection  
- Detects anomalies in claims  
- Validates location and activity  

### ⭐ Safe Zone Recommendation (Key Innovation)  
- Suggests better working areas  
- Helps workers **avoid income loss proactively**
